The Solana Clawd

A lobster claw companion that lives in your macOS menu bar. It sees your screen, speaks with a lobster voice, writes code, and connects to Solana.

Step 1
Push to Talk

Hold Ctrl+Option to activate the microphone. Your voice is captured by AVAudioEngine and streamed to AssemblyAI in real-time.

Step 2
Screenshot + Claude Vision

A screenshot is captured via ScreenCaptureKit and sent alongside the transcript to Claude, which sees and understands your screen.

Step 3
Claw Points at UI Elements

Claude embeds [CLAW:x,y:label:screenN] tags in responses. The lobster claw flies along a bezier arc to point at the referenced element.
